1.Sarada Uchiha




Sarada Uchiha is a kunoichi from Konohagakure's Uchiha clan. Because she grew up

without having her father around, Sarada initially struggles to understand who she

is or what she's supposed to be. After meeting him with the help of Naruto Uzumaki,

Sarada learns that she is defined by the connections she has with others. As a

member of Team Konohamaru, she seeks to someday become Hokage so that she

can connect with as many people as possible.
